About App

Last Updated: February 21, 2025


This app helps you easily find the nearest smart trash and recycling bins in your area. Simply open the app to locate the closest bin and see what types of waste each bin accepts.

Key Features:

  • Bin Locator: Quickly find the nearest recycling and trash bins using your device's GPS.
  • Waste Type Guide: See what types of waste each bin accepts (e.g., biodegradable, non-biodegradable, recyclable).
  • Distance Display: View the distance to each bin in meters.
  • App Updates: Stay informed about app updates and improvements.

How to Use:

  • 1) Open the app and allow access to your location.
  • 2) The app will automatically display the nearest bins.
  • 3) Tap on a bin to see its location on a map and the types of waste it accepts.
